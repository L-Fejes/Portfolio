about me

I'm 23 years old studying Video Game Design and Development

As Partrick Rothfuss once said, "Create a different world, then tell a story in it." I've been playing games as long as I can remember, and the games that stuck with me were the one's that drew me into their rich environments. Skyrim, Fallout New Vegas, Bioshock Infinite, and DeadSpace are all games that are all very meaningful to me because they are games with incredibly flushed out and atmospheric worlds. They are games that inspire me improve my own work.

what i offer

Level Design

I've worked with tilesets to build Platformers, I've used both Unity 3D, and Unreal build both large scale environments and cramped corridors. I've done lighting design, and I've built unique 3D and 2D assets to populate my worlds.

Camera Controllers

Building camera's is something that I enjoy in relation to Level Building, it also has a lot to due with the aesthetic experience of a game. It's a very jarring experince to clip through a wall, or you're view starts stuttering trying to keep up with the action.

Mobile Development

I've built platformer's in Android Studio, and I've recently been working with XCode and Swift to build for iOs, I have a lot to learn, but mobile is a huge market, and I have always been a fan of having games on the go.

AI Controllers

An AI controller was one of the first things I built indipendantly, it was a Super Mario 3 build, and I just loved getting to bring the enemies to life. There is a slight sadistic pleasure in knowing you contributed to ruining a player's run, and I love doing it.
